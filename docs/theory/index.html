The Central Insight

The Miracle of High Dimensionality: In spaces with 512+ dimensions, randomly generated vectors are almost certainly orthogonal to each other. This means you can create millions of distinct concepts without any coordination‚Äîjust generate random vectors, and they're guaranteed to be unique.

Why This Matters for AGI

Spock AGISystem2 bridges the gap between neural flexibility and symbolic rigor. Unlike pure neural networks (black boxes) or pure symbolic systems (brittle logic), Spock provides:

  • Continuous reasoning ‚Äì No discrete jumps; smooth transitions through concept space
  • Compositional structure ‚Äì Complex knowledge built from simple operations
  • Native uncertainty ‚Äì Partial truths and degrees of belief are geometric, not bolted on
